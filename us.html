WE ARE SUNNY WANDERERS!


We are four Chinese students from Centre for Advanced Spatial Analysis (CASA) in University College London (UCL). We are all Beijing-lovers and curious about many phenomena in any aspects of Beijing. As school district housing is a very popular topic in Beijing, we decide to make use of data to explore such interesting issue.
